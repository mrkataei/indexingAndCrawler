Publisher and mobile game developer Thumbage Co., Ltd. announced that its mobile game Destiny Child: Defense War took the #2 and #21 spot on Google Play Store’s Top New Free Game Strategy charts in Singapore and Australia on the 5th of Nov.
In addition to its #2 place in Singapore, the game is ranked #25 in Google Play Store’s Top new free games.

Notably, the game is gradually gaining momentum through its dynamic gameplay and the positive feedback from players.

Destiny Child: Defense War is a defense game that utilizes the IP(intellectual property) of Destiny Child. The player becomes an Archfiend Candidate and will, in battle, challenge for the highest honor.

The player can summon Destiny Child’s characters and defend the Archfiend. All the favorite characters from the original Destiny Child game can be summoned to guard the Archfiend, such as Davi, Mona, Lisa, and Frej!
Players can battle in real-time matches with players from all over the world. Players can battle with friends or clan members.
Destiny Child: Defense War is expected to have its official launch by the end of the year.
